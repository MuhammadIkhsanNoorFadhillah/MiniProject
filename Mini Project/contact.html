Honda CB750 Cafe Racer

The Honda CB750 is an iconic and special motorbike, even getting the nickname "Original Superbike". Apart from that, the Honda CB750 is also often relied on for custom projects by many builders because of its 749cc 4 cylinder engine.

Mean Green Custom Street750

Aditya Deshmukh started Mean Green Customs back in 2014 in Mumbai, India. The workshop has been busy the last few years turning out several tasty customs. This meant he had to wait before he could divert his attention to his latest personal project, a heavily customised Harley Davidson Street 750.

Kawasaki Z1000 CafeRacer

With its super cool looks and attractive body appearance, who would have thought that the base is the Kawasaki Z1000. This motorbike, which can be said to be cafe racer style, is the product of Sur Les Chapeaux De Roues (SLCDR), France. Uniquely, they admitted that this modification was inspired by Cadillac cars from the 1970s.
